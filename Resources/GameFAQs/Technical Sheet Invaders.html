Invaders

Particles

  • The total power of all particles dropped by a single invader.
  • Not affected by rank, affinity or type.
  • The number of actual blue, green and gold particles can vary, but will always add up to the corresponding total power.

For all invader locations below, rotate the invader 180 first before determining where the coordinates apply to. In other words, the invaders are facing the same way as your ship.

Note that while the figures are accurate, it is hard to determine the actual center [0, 0] of the invaders since the invader images may not be accurately cropped to size. Use these numbers to compare in relative to each other, rather than taking them as an absolute value.

If you download the invader image assets below, note that the actual image resolutions of the armor and shield assets are massively upscaled to the actual image resolution size of their respective invaders. This is such that the armored and shielded variants of the respective invaders look correct, using the oversize ratio of the armor and shield. Thus, the actual resolution of the armor and shield assets are actually much lower. However, the resolution of the invader shadows are true to the extracted resolution, since the scaling of the shadows aren't really set in proportion to the invaders themselves in the game files.
